ABOUT FLIPZERO

FlipZero firmly believes that all business security risks should be assessed and addressed. Small & Midsize businesses forgo proper security measures due to the exuberant costs being placed on security and risk services.

FlipZero focuses on assisting these organizations. No one should go without proper security because of incredible costs or lack of knowledge.

Small & Midsize businesses requires data security, monitoring, auditing, and protection of enterprise data to help ensure compliance.

Small & Midsize businesses are faced with data breaches, ransomware attacks. SMB Businesses need to safeguard diverse data types such as structured and unstructured, online and offline, and must do so across complex systems including cloud and onsite data environments.
